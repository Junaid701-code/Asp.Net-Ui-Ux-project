Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how Traxccel (Traxcel LLC) handles your information gathered through various interactions with our website(www.traxccel.com) all of which are collectively referred to as “Services”. By engaging with our website, you are acknowledging your consent to the terms outlined in this privacy policy.



Information collection and use

We would like to let you know that whenever you visit our website, we gather information from your browser called "Log Data." This log data may contain information on the Internet Protocol ("IP") your address, the version of your browser, the pages you visit on our website, the time and date of your visit, the amount of time you spend on web pages, and other statistics.

We may require certain personally identifiable information from you to improve your experience using our service. This information includes but is only limited to your name, phone number, email address, company name, and postal address. The information we gather will be used for several purposes such as contacting you, confirming your identity, sharing promotional communications, messages and sending emails.

You may at any time make a request discontinuation of receiving communications, emails or other correspondence.

Information retention

We keep your personal data for as long as it is legitimately required for the objectives outlined in this privacy policy. In order to enforce our agreements, handle disputes, and fulfill our legal and professional requirements, we may also need to keep your personal information on record.

Cookies

Cookies are used by our website to gather data and improve our services. You can choose to accept or reject these cookies, and you will be notified when one is sent to you. You might not be able to use some parts of our service if you decide to reject our cookies.

Information sharing

We may disclose your personal information with third parties for legitimate business-related purposes; or those who provide services to us. However, importantly they are under strict obligation not to reveal or utilize your personal information for any other purposes.

Additionally, if required by law, by a regulator, or during legal processes, we might also need to disclose your personal information.

Security

We appreciate your trust in us for providing your personal information; therefore, we employ industry-standard measures to safeguard your data. However, it is important for us to inform you that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage provides 100% security and reliability, so we are unable to guarantee complete security.

Links to external websites

There may be links to external websites on our service. A third-party link will take you to that website when you click on it. Please be aware that we do not run these external websites. As a result, we highly suggest that you read these websites' privacy policies. Regarding any third-party websites or services, we have no control over and take no liability for their practices, privacy policies, or content.

Children's privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not intentionally gather personally identifiable information about children younger than thirteen. If we find out that someone under the age of 13 has given us personal information, we promptly remove it from our servers. Please get in contact with us if you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has given us personal information so that we can take the appropriate action.

Changes to this privacy policy

Our privacy policy may undergo changes occasionally. As a result, we suggest that you review this page for updates periodically. Any modifications will be posted on this page, where you may find the updated privacy policy. As soon as the changes are published on this website, they take effect without delay.
